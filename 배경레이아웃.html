신비로운 환상 속의 검은 모래

This sand is sometimes referred to as mystical sand.
Because black sand is black rather than
the yellowish glow we normally see, looking at it
can give the illusion of falling into a black hole.

검은 모래는 어떻게 만들어질까요?

Unlike ordinary sand, black sand is rare
and mysterious because black basalt
is carved into the waves
